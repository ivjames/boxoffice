Everything in one place

A box office that does the whole job

Browse & buy

General-admission or reserved-seat performances, live availability, a held cart with a countdown timer, and Stripe Checkout.

Tickets that scan

Every paid order is emailed QR tickets automatically. Door staff scan and redeem them from the dashboard — no separate app to install.

A staff dashboard

Role-gated event and performance management, pricing tiers, order lookup, and sales reporting — everything your team needs behind one login.

Seating charts

Draw reserved-seat maps with sections and zones, or keep it simple with general admission. Buyers pick real seats; you keep control of the house.

Scan at the door

A camera-based scanner runs right in the browser on any phone or tablet. Valid, already-used, and unknown tickets are called out instantly.

Sales you can see

Track orders and revenue per event and performance, so you know how a run is selling while there's still time to do something about it.

How it works

Live in three steps

  1. 1

    Set up your venue

    We spin up your branded storefront on your own subdomain, with your colors and logo. Add your team and their roles.

  2. 2

    Publish your events

    Create events and performances, draw your seating chart or set general admission, and price your tiers. Publish when you're ready.

  3. 3

    Sell & scan

    Buyers check out through Stripe and get QR tickets by email. On show night, your staff scans them at the door from any phone.
